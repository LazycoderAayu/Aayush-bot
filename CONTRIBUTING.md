## üìù Guidelines in Brief
1. **Efficiency First:** Please check existing issues and pull requests to avoid duplicate efforts.
2. **Community Collaboration:** For significant changes or new logic features, we encourage you to discuss your ideas in the [dev channel] first.
3. **Draft Pull Requests:** For ongoing work on major features, please open a Draft PR to keep the team informed of your progress.
4. **Code Quality:** Adhere to the [Code Guidelines]. We use TypeScript to ensure type safety and long-term maintainability.
5. **Clear Documentation:** When submitting a PR, please provide a clear explanation of your changes and the "why" behind them.
6. **Stability:** Please test and validate your changes thoroughly. We aim to keep the main branch stable at all times.
7. **Consistency:** To maintain a unified codebase, please avoid purely stylistic changes that don't improve functionality.
8. **Holistic Contributions:** We highly value contributions beyond code, such as improving documentation, refining system prompts, or reporting bugs.
